Problem

An opportunity existed to re-develop CoreSites external marketing website www.coresite.com to reflect the updated mission of CoreSite as well as to revamp the latest web branding, web technology, and create a responsive, intuitive, user-friendly design. Upon completion of the development of the site, CoreSite needed to assume primary responsibility for website content maintenance and administration so the CMS had to be user friendly, fast, and stable. CoreSite’s primary objective was to redesign the CoreSite website to build awareness, interest, and conversion through a re-focused Information architecture and top of the line bespoke user experience.

Solution

The SVZ team delivered an all inclusive redesign & development of the entire current CoreSite.com website while honoring their current brand guidelines, this included Pre-build strategy, Information Architecture, UX Design, Visual Development (Webflow), Custom Webflow CMS Development, Advanced Bespoke Animation & Interaction Design, Best SEO & Code Practices, and finally, CMS Onboarding + Training Videos upon hand-off.

What is an RFP?

A request for proposal (RFP) is a great way for you to quickly get us up to speed on your project. It should include an introduction to your company and the reason for submitting your RFP. Tell us what it is you hope to accomplish with us and lay out any problems you or your users face with your current site. Lastly, please provide an overview of your project scope, timeline, and budget. We're happy to guide you through this on a call.
